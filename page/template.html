5. Community fishing area management plan

A. Vision

The community fisheries has the vision of participating in managing and using fisheries resources equitably and sustainably in order to contribute to reduction of the poverty of the people.

B. Objectives

  • Participate in managing freshwater fishing grounds where fishing lots have been cancelled or parts of fishing lots released, family fishing areas that are set aside for people to fish traditionally or for communities to fish, reserve fishing grounds, flood- plains during the flood season, flooded forest and mangrove forest areas, and coastal fishing grounds.
  • Participate in managing fisheries resources sustainably and ensure the equitable sharing of benefits from fisheries resources among Khmer citizens.
  • To improve the living situation of members of the community fisheries through participatory integrated development.
  • So that Khmer citizens will clearly understand and begin to recognize that they are the owners of natural resources.

D. Strategy

In order to implement the plan successfully, the following strategies have been developed:

  • Set up a committee to implement, follow up, monitor, and evaluate implementation of the plan, including: members of the community fisheries committee, elders, and community fisheries members who have good experience and knowledge, particularly with regards to fisheries issues.
  • Request technical assistance from the Fisheries Administration with jurisdiction so that they can offer opinions on any plans that relate to fisheries expertise, such as: planning for fish brood stock conservation areas, establishing flood forest conserva- tion areas and biodiversity conservation areas, digging out channels, determining fishing gears, raising fish, fundraising, etc.
  • Develop a strategy for communication with competent institutions, organizations, projects, and donors in order to get their support and funds for the process of imple- menting the plan.
  • Preparing specific programs for implementing each stage of the plan
  • Contact the Fisheries Administration to request funds and request that they help find partners to support the community fisheries management plan.
  • Raise awareness on the community fishing area management plan among commu- nity fisheries members and important stakeholders in order to encourage participation in and support for implementation of the management plan
  • Encourage community fisheries members to make contributions
  • Develop means to deal with fishers from outside the community
  • Build the technical and management capacity of the community fisheries committee Organize groups of fisheries according to the type of gear they use, to participate in implementation of the plan
  • Include the community fisheries management plan in the commune/ sangkat development plan.
